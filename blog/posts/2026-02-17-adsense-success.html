Mission Accomplished: AdSense & New Infrastructure!

Tech Update Milestone

Hi everyone! James here with some exciting tech news about the website. Today, we successfully completed a major migration of my entire project to a brand-new GitHub repository and established a lightning-fast deployment pipeline to Cloudflare Pages.

Fixing the AdSense Issue

One of the biggest hurdles lately was getting Google AdSense to work correctly. Even though the account was "Ready," some pages were missing the code. I've now verified that every single page—from my basketball stories to my Minecraft guides—has the correct script in the header.

What This Means for the Future

With this new setup, I can share my stories and projects faster than ever. Every time I make a change, it automatically gets backed up to GitHub and published to the world via Cloudflare.

  • Reliability: The site is now safer and more stable.
  • Growth: I can focus more on content and less on fixing bugs.
  • Learning: Setting this up taught me a lot about Git, tokens, and web hosting!

Thanks for following my journey. I can't wait to show you what's coming next!

Stay creative!
